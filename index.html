Distress Rating Guide

  • 0–3Little or no anxiety — start here
  • 4–6Moderate anxiety — work up to these
  • 7–10High anxiety — tackle last

Exposure Tips

  • Stay in each situation until anxiety drops by half
  • Repeat each rung several times before moving up
  • Avoid safety behaviours (distraction, escape)
  • Anxiety always peaks, then falls — trust the process
  • Celebrate every completed rung, big or small

Safety & Support

When to seek professional help

⚠️ Important disclaimer: FearFade is not a substitute for professional therapy. If your phobia causes severe distress, significantly impacts daily life, or is accompanied by panic disorder or depression, please consult a licensed mental health professional.

A trained therapist can provide personalised CBT, EMDR, or other evidence-based treatments for phobias.
