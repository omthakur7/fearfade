Self-Help Tools

Practice calm, anytime

Dedicated tools to help you regulate your nervous system before, during, or after exposure practice.

🌬️ Box Breathing (4-4-4-4)

Breathe IN for 4 counts… HOLD for 4… OUT for 4… HOLD for 4. Repeat 4 times. Ideal for calming before an exposure session.

🌿 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Name 5 things you see → 4 you touch → 3 you hear → 2 you smell → 1 you taste. Brings you back to the present moment instantly.

💪 TIPP Skill

Temperature — cold water on face. Intense exercise 60 sec. Paced breathing. Progressive relaxation.

🧘 Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Starting at your feet, tense each muscle group for 5 seconds, then release. Work up to your face. Full body relaxation in ~10 minutes.

Safety & Support

When to seek professional help

⚠️ Important disclaimer: FearFade is not a substitute for professional therapy. If your phobia causes severe distress, significantly impacts daily life, or is accompanied by panic disorder or depression, please consult a licensed mental health professional.

A trained therapist can provide personalised CBT, EMDR, or other evidence-based treatments for phobias.
